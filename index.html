How to Demo

Here are the modules and steps involved in this demo

1

Video Source CH1

CH1 is from MTR's rtsp. The Ngnix streaming server transcodes the rtsp to rtpm through ffmpeg. The rtmp is then transcoded to FLV and HLS for displaying web. Note that FLV transcoding speed is faster than HLS.
2

Video Source CH3

User pushes video stream from any rtmp streaming app on mobile or desktop (i.e. OBS) through rtmp://66.203.157.36:31935/flv03/mydemo3 with mydemo3 being the "stream key". This video is transcoded to FLV for web display
3

FLV vs HLS

FLV has less delay than HLS, however, HLS can be displayed on most major web browers while FLV cannot be displayed on Safari
